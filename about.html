Hey, nice to meet you.

I was drawn to design through my close involvement of helping people interact with technology whilst working as a trainer at Apple. I saw tech through the eyes of old and new users. I sat with people through highs and lows. I was fortunate enough to spend time working with a number of visually impaired customers and discovered the need for inclusive design first-hand.

I approach design with the aim of creating delightful and intuitive experiences through a deep understanding of problems.

I love being part of every stage in the process; defining, designing and refining, keeping the person on the other side of the screen in mind every step of the way.
