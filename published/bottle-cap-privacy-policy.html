Bottle Cap Privacy Policy

TL;DR

Your data never leaves your device. Bottle Cap reads and writes health data exclusively from, and to HealthKit on your device. All your data is saved on your iCloud.

Health Data

We use Apple's APIs to read and/or write your alcohol consumption to the Apple Health app on your device. This data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor do we collect any personal information.

This app cannot read from or write to Apple Health without your consent.
